Off-Piste Technique Course

For advanced skiers.

You're already an experienced skier and you love venturing off piste, but you want to improve your technique so that you can cope with all kinds of terrain with greater confidence.

Vivid Mountain has teamed up with a specialist instructor for a dedicated Off-Piste Course which is available during the La Clusaz week, January 8th to January 15th 2016. The resort offers plenty and varied opportunities for off-piste skiing. Your experienced instructor will not only improve your technique but also introduce you to the thrills of off-piste skiing, away from the crowds. You will receive 4 full days of instruction during the week, leaving you 2 days of free skiing.

Security is of course our priority. Your instructor has intimate knowledge of the resort and the choice of mountain will depend on the conditions on any given day. The group will be limited to 6 participants and each skier will be equipped with a security pack, comprising transceiver, shovel and probe. Our instructors will always endeavour to find the best off-piste available, but if weather or safety conditions mean that off-piste skiing is not possible, we will arrange alternative on-piste technique sessions. Whilst we do not offer a snow guarantee, La Clusaz has a good historical snow record.
