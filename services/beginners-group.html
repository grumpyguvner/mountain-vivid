Beginners' group

For anyone new to skiing.

If you enjoy the mountains, but have never been skiing why not give it a try? We have partnered with a team of experienced ski instructors to offer a dedicated Beginners' Group, specifically for Vivid Mountain. The group is limited to between 4 and 6 participants so that each gets the attention needed to progress quickly.

Unlike with most ski schools, you will be taken care of by your Vivid Mountain instructor for the 6 days: 3 hours of instruction in the morning, a table booked for lunch and 2 hours of instruction in the afternoon. This ensures that you have a great time from the start to the end of your holiday and don't have to spend time going down the same green run again and again because you are unsure of your way around the mountain. Being coached in a small intensive group will help you make rapid progress and your instructor will even book you the best spots for lunch (where you can meet up with skiers from other groups).

Our Beginners' Group is available during the Les Saisies week, Friday 22nd– Friday 29th January 2016. The resort is ideally suited to beginners and those who've not yet experienced the Alps will marvel at its stunning scenery.

As our Beginners' Group is only one of the groups in Les Saisies, it's perfect for skiers bringing a partner or friend along who has never been on the slopes. During the day, you ski in groups appropriate to your level knowing that the beginners are in the capable hands of an experienced instructor. Then everyone meets for drinks and dinner at tables reserved for Vivid Mountain. Equally, if you are a solo traveller skiing for the first time, you can socialise with a wider group after skiing.

Price: £475, including ski and boot hire
