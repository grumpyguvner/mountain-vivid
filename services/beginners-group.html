Beginners' group

For anyone new to skiing.

If you enjoy the mountains, but have never been skiing why not give it a try? We have partnered with a team of experienced ski instructors to offer a dedicated Beginners' group, specifically for Vivid Mountain. A group is limited to 4 participants so that each gets the attention needed to progress quickly.

Our Beginners' Group is available during the Les Saisies week, January 15th to 22nd January 2016. The resort is ideally suited to beginners and those who've not yet experienced the Alps will marvel at its stunning scenery.

As our Beginners' Group is only one of the groups in Les Saisies, it's perfect for skiers bringing a partner or friend along who has never been on the slopes. During the day, you ski in groups appropriate to your level and then everyone meets for drinks and dinner. Equally, if you are a solo traveller skiing for the first time, you can socialise with a wider group after skiing.
