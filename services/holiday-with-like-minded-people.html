Holiday with like-minded people

Discerning gay people who love the mountains.

On a Vivid Mountain holiday you will be with active, discerning gay people who share a love of the mountains. Our guests come from all walks of life but they come on holiday with us to have amazing experiences in a beautiful mountain environment with other gay people. Skiing is such a sociable sport and with a maximum of 20 people on any one trip everyone quickly gets to know one another. We will also make it easy for everyone to ski with people of a similar ability, ensuring everyone has a great time on the mountain.
