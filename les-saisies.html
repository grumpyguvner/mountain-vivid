Les Saisies (1650m)

Ideal for intermediate skiers and beginners.

The wide, tree-lined slopes of Les Saisies and the six connected resorts of the ‘Espace Diamant’ are ideal for intermediate skiers and beginners. At 1650m and benefitting from its own micro-climate, Les Saisies is one of the most snow-sure resorts in the region offerering extensive skiing and open plateaus with stunning views across to Mont Blanc.

Les Saisies, lined with traditional chalets, has a range of shops, bars and restaurants offering a selection of local produce, and has an easy transfer time of XXXX from Geneva.
