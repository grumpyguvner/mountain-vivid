Great Resorts

We use some of the best resorts.

We ski in authentic and charming resorts with a good snow record and easy access to high altitude skiing - like La Clusaz, Morzine and Les Saisies.

La Clusaz has five interlinked areas of fantastic and diverse skiing – from pretty tree-lined blues to steep and challenging blacks. The wide, tree-lined slopes of Les Saisies and the six connected resorts of the 'Espace Diamant' are ideal for intermediate skiers and beginners. While Morzine is part of the famous Portes du Soleil which covers over 650km of piste, spans two countries and includes well-known resorts like Avoriaz, Les Gets and Châtel. The Portes Du Soleil is an intermediate skier’s heaven!

Our resorts are never more than 110km from Geneva airport so under normal weather and travel conditions the transfer time is less than two hours.

If you are new to skiing try our Beginners' Group, or if you want to be able to ski the whole mountain with style and ease, why not book our Off-Piste Technique Group.
