Morzine (1000m - 2465m)

Part of the Portes du Soleil, an intermediate skier's heaven.

Morzine is part of the famous Portes du Soleil which covers over 650km of piste, spans two countries and includes well-known resorts like Avoriaz, Les Gets and Châtel. The Portes Du Soleil is an intermediate skier’s heaven with lots of challenging red runs and plenty of great skiing for every standard of skier.

Morzine is a traditional alpine town situated in a tree-lined valley. It offers a wide variety of bars, shops and activities, and has an easy transfer time of XXXX from Geneva.

Hotel Champs-Fleuris 4*

The Champs-Fleuris is a family-run hotel which marries traditional warmth with contemporary comfort. It is perfectly situated at the foot of the pistes and opposite the Pleney télécabine.

At the end of a great days skiing you can relax in the large outdoor, heated pool, sensory spa pool, steam room or sauna; and socialise in the spacious and comfortable bar and lounge area.

The dining room has a cosy mountain feel to it. It offers an extensive breakfast buffet and dinners are themed around different cuisines, including regional dishes.
