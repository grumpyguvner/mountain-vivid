FAQs:

  1. Are other dates available?
    Yes, please call us to discuss your requirement. A minimum group size of six is required.
  2. Can I travel by train?
    Yes, of course. You can either tie-in with the transfer from Geneva airport or we can arrange a bespoke transfers.
  3. I'm booking with a group of friends. Will we be able to sit together at dinner?
    Our holidays are great for spending time with old friends and making new ones! To ensure everyone feels welcome we all eat together at tables reserved for Vivid Mountain.
  4. Can I book just one place?
    Yes! Our friendly and welcoming holidays are ideal for skiers travelling on their own. We offer single rooms (at a supplement) or can pair you in a twin if there are others who want to share.
  5. I have never skied before.
    Our Beginners' Group in Les Saisies is perfect for you.
  6. Will I be able to ski with people of my ability?
    We ascertain the level that our guests ski at (check your ski level) before your holiday, and suggest groups of similar abilities on the first evening. You can choose to ski with our suggested group or make your own plans.
