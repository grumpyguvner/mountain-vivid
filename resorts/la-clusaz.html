La Clusaz (1100m - 2600m)

Great skiing against a stunning backdrop.

La Clusaz offers great skiing for all levels set against a backdrop of stunning natural beauty. La Clusaz ski area has five interlinked areas of fantastic and diverse skiing – from pretty tree-lined blues to steep and challenging blacks. An easy bus ride links La Clusaz to nearby Le Grand Bornand, a picturesque and unspoilt skiing area. The two resorts make up the Aravis area, offering 220km of varied skiing.

La Clusaz has one of the prettiest French ski villages at its heart and has a wide range of shops and bars and has an easy transfer time of 75 minutes from Geneva (in normal weather and traffic conditions).
