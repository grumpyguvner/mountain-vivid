Morzine (1000m - 2465m)

At the heart of the Portes du Soleil.

Morzine is part of the famous Portes du Soleil which covers over 650km of piste, spans two countries and includes well-known resorts like Avoriaz, Les Gets and Châtel. The Portes du Soleil offers something for everyone with plenty of great skiing for all standards of skier, including lots of challenging red runs.

Morzine is a traditional alpine town situated in a tree-lined valley. It offers a wide variety of bars, shops and activities, and has an easy transfer time of 80 minutes from Geneva.

Activities

Nightlife

A lively resort with lots to do. Favourites include the Dixie Bar, Le Crépuscule, Rhodos, Tibetan and the Bowling Bar.
