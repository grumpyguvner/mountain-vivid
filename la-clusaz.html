La Clusaz (1100m - 2600m)

Great skiing against a stunning backdrop.

La Clusaz offers 220km of great skiing for all levels set against a backdrop of stunning natural beauty. The Aravis ski area comprises five interlinked areas of fantastic and diverse skiing, from pretty tree lined blues to steep and challenging blacks. An easy bus ride links La Clusaz to nearby Le Grand Bornand, a picturesque and unspoilt skiing area.

La Clusaz has one of the prettiest French ski villages at its heart and has a wide range of shops and bars.
