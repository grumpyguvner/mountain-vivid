Hotel Champs Fleuris 4*

Family run, traditional warmth, contemporary comfort.

The Champs-Fleuris is a family-run hotel which marries traditional warmth with contemporary comfort. It is perfectly situated at the foot of the pistes and opposite the Pléney télécabine.

The Hotel Champs Fleuris is in the resort of Morzine, read more about Morzine.

  • At the end of a great days skiing relaxing shouldn't be a problem as you have free access to a large outdoor, heated pool, sensory spa pool, steam room or sauna.
  • Spacious and comfortable bar and lounge area.
  • The dining room has a cosy mountain feel to it. It offers an extensive breakfast buffet and dinners are themed around different cuisines, including regional dishes.
  • The rooms are decorated using local wood and fabrics and have plenty of storage space.
  • Each room has an ensuite with bath and a balcony with mountain views.
  • Hotel facilities: covered swimming pool; wellness centre with sensorial pool, sauna, hammam, massage (bookings direct with hotel); pool table, free wifi, lift.
  • Room facilities: ensuite with bath; 8 English-speaking TV channels; telephone; safe; hairdryer
