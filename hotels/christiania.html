Hotel Christiania 2*

Very close to the slopes, this is a comfortable hotel offering good value.

Situated in the heart of La Clusaz and very close to the slopes, this is a comfortable hotel offering good value. The rooms are individually decorated and ensuite bathrooms have either a bath or shower.

The Hotel Christiania is in the resort of La Clusaz, read more about La Clusaz.

  • The hotel has a relaxed atmosphere with a bar and popular restaurant, Le Bistro.
  • Accommodation is on a half-board basis with a buffet breakfast and 3-course dinner including wine with the meal.
  • We have arranged for two evening meals in outside restaurants: one in the popular Auberge d’Aravis and the other in the 4* Hotel Beauregard restaurant, usually open only to its own hotel residents.
  • Hotel facilities: free Wifi; lift; ski store room
  • Bedroom facilities; ensuite bathroom; TV and telephone.

Dates and prices

We will be staying at the Hotel Christiania in La Clusaz from March 6th 2016 to March 13th 2016 with prices starting at £1145 per person.
