Hotel Beauregard 4*

Ideally situated at the foot of the slopes.

Ideally situated at the foot of the slopes and within 100m of a new chair-télémix lift, Hotel Beauregard really is ski-in/ski-out. A large traditional Savoyard chalet type building, the hotel's rooms are all decorated in typical alpine style and each one has a balcony looking out on the Aravis mountains.

The Hotel Beauregard is in the resort of La Clusaz, read more about La Clusaz.

  • You will find spacious bar and seating areas with comfortable armchairs.
  • Complimentary facilities include a superb indoor swimming pool, a relaxing jacuzzi, steam room and sauna.
  • The ski room is serviced by a technician who is at hand to help you with your equipment at the beginning and end of each day.
  • An elegant hotel with attentive staff that is popular with discerning guests who appreciate its spacious communal areas as well as the comfort of its rooms.
  • All the shops, bars and facilities of La Clusaz village are an easy walk away.
