A comfortable, alpine-style hotel offering individually styled rooms, all with ensuite facilities.

The Hotel Le Very is in the resort of Les Saisies, read more about Les Saisies.

  • Breakfast is provided at the adjoining Le Copon restaurant which also has a cosy bar, ideal for after-ski or pre-dinner drinks.
  • In Les Saisies we will be offering two dinner venues: at Le Copon and at Tante Annick which is a five-minute walk from the hotel. In each case, a three-course meal with drinks will be provided.
  • Le Very hotel has a jacuzzi and sauna available for those who would like to book these facilities separately.
  • The ski rental shop is a short walk from the hotel.
  • Your skis and boots will be stored overnight in heated lockers and you can ski direct in and out from the shop to the pistes.
  • Prices are per person sharing a twin bedded room with an ensuite shower.
  • All rooms have telephone and TV.

Dates and prices

We will be staying at the Hotel Le Very in Les Saisies from January 22nd 2016 to January 29th 2016 with prices starting at £995 per person.
